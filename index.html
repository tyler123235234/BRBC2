Welcome

Welcome to Brookville Road Baptist Church in Bath, Indiana. We are a Bible-believing church dedicated to glorifying God, sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ, encouraging Christian growth, and serving our community with compassion and love. Whether you are looking for a church home or visiting for the first time, we invite you to worship with us.
